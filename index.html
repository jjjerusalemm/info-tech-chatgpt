Overview

Why ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is currently one of the most visible AI tools in the world. Our group chose it because students, businesses, and regular users are already relying on it for writing, coding, and problem-solving. It is a clear example of how Generative AI is transitioning from a new trend to a mainstream tool.

Why ChatGPT is important

  • Delivers instant, 24/7 responses
  • Enhances productivity in work, school, and research
  • Supports Creativity (ideas, content, brainstorming, and writing)
  • Automates repetetive tasks and saves time
  • Helps users learn complex topics in simple explanations
  • Accessible to anyone with internet access
  • continuously improving through model updates

Industries already using ChatGPT

  • Education: Tutoring, study help, writing support
  • Healthcare: summarizing medical notes, patient communication
  • Business: customer service, emails, data analysis
  • Tech: debugging, documentation, coding assistance
  • Creative fields: scriptwriting, marketing content, idea generation
Technology

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a conversational AI system created by OpenAI. It is built on a large language model that was trained on huge amounts of text from books, articles, and websites. The model predicts the next word in a sentence, one word at a time, which allows it to respond to questions, summaries, and prompts in a natural way.

It does not “think” like a human. Instead, it uses patterns in data to generate likely responses – which is powerful, but sometimes inaccurate.

How it works

How ChatGPT works

At a high level, ChatGPT:

  • Takes the user’s text input and converts it into numeric tokens.
  • Runs those tokens through many layers of a neural network that has learned patterns from training data.
  • Predicts the most likely next token over and over, generating a full response.
  • Uses techniques like reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) to make answers safer and more helpful.
